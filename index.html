About Numerology

Numerology is an ancient mystical science that reveals the hidden meanings behind numbers. It helps us understand our life purpose, talents, and potential challenges through the analysis of our birth date and name.

This calculator uses traditional numerology methods to determine your core numbers, including your Life Path Number, Destiny Number, and Soul Urge Number.

Understanding Your Numbers

Life Path Number

Calculated from your birth date, this number reveals your life's purpose and the path you're destined to follow.

Destiny Number

Derived from your full name, this number indicates your natural talents and the goals you're meant to achieve.

Soul Urge Number

Based on the vowels in your name, this number reveals your inner desires and what truly motivates you.
